# 🇨🇮 Ivory Coast - E-Invoicing Specifications (SIGF)

**Status:** 🟡 **Partial** | Mandatory for digital services since 2019
**Authority:** DGI (Direction Générale des Impôts)
**Platform:** SIGF (Système Intégré de Gestion de la Fiscalité)

---

## 1. Context & Overview

Ivory Coast implemented a certified e-invoicing system in 2019 as part of the National Development Plan (PND). Currently, the mandate primarily affects technology companies offering digital goods or services, with plans for broader extension.

| Date | Scope | Obligation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **2019** | Digital service providers | Mandatory e-invoicing |
| **Planned** | All businesses | Extended mandate (timeline TBD) |

---

## 2. Technical Requirements

### Platform
- **SIGF** (Integrated Tax Management System)
- Real-time invoice transmission
- DGI central platform

### Format Requirements
- National XML format
- Digital signature
- Structured invoice data

---

## 3. Key Requirements

- **NIF** (Numéro d'Identification Fiscale)
- DGI registration
- Certified e-invoicing solution
- Real-time data transmission
- Invoice validation

---

## 4. Business Model

- **Clearance Model:** Invoices sent to DGI
- **Real-time Reporting:** Immediate transmission required
- Partial scope (digital economy first)
