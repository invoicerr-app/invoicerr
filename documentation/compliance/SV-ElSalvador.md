# 🇸🇻 El Salvador - E-Invoicing Specifications

**Status:** 🟢 **Mandatory** | Active
**Authority:** MH (Ministerio de Hacienda)
**Platform:** MH Electronic Invoice System

---

## 1. Context & Overview

El Salvador has mandatory e-invoicing (factura electrónica) through the Ministry of Hacienda. All businesses must issue electronic invoices authorized by the tax authority.

---

## 2. Technical Requirements

### Platform
- **MH Electronic Invoice System**
- API web services
- Certificación de contribuyentes

### Format Requirements
- **XML format** (national schema)
- Digital signature
- MH standard

---

## 3. Implementation Timeline

| Date | Scope | Obligation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **2018+** | Progressive rollout | Mandatory e-invoicing |
| **Ongoing** | All businesses | Continuous MH authorization |

---

## 4. Business Model

- **Clearance Model:** Pre-authorization required
- Sequential numbering
- Digital certificate required
- Real-time validation
