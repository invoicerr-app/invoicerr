## 1. Context & Overview

Angola is the only African country (outside Europe) to have adopted the OECD SAF-T (Standard Audit File for Tax) requirements for electronic data transmission. Since 2019, the AGT collects accounting and invoice data from selected taxpayers through this system.

| Date | Scope | Obligation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **2019** | Selected taxpayers | SAF-T reporting mandatory |
| **Ongoing** | Progressive rollout | Extended to more businesses |
