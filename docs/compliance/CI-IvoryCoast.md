## 1. Context & Overview

Ivory Coast implemented a certified e-invoicing system in 2019 as part of the National Development Plan (PND). Currently, the mandate primarily affects technology companies offering digital goods or services, with plans for broader extension.

| Date | Scope | Obligation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **2019** | Digital service providers | Mandatory e-invoicing |
| **Planned** | All businesses | Extended mandate (timeline TBD) |
