## 1. Context & Overview

Morocco has implemented mandatory e-invoicing through the DGI. All businesses must issue electronic invoices authorized by the tax authority.

---

## 2. Technical Requirements

### Platform
- **DGI e-Invoice System**
- API integration
- Online portal

### Format Requirements
- **XML format** (national schema)
- Digital signature
- DGI standard

---

## 3. Implementation Timeline

| Date | Scope | Obligation |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **2020+** | Large enterprises | Progressive mandatory rollout |
| **2024+** | All businesses | Full mandatory e-invoicing |

---

## 4. Business Model

- **Clearance Model:** Tax authority authorization
- Centralized reporting
- Local certification

## 5. Key Requirements

- ICE (Identifiant Commun de l'Entreprise)
- DGI certification
- XML format
- Digital signature
